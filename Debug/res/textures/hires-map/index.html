Bluemarble high-resolution map of the earth

Below is a scaled-down version of the full bluemarble image of the earth. You can click on it to see a part of it in full resolution. With javascript enabled, a red rectangle will follow your mousepointer, indicating the exact part that will be shown on clicking. When combined into a single image the full resolution would be 43200x21600 pixels (this is about 1km on earth per pixel).
Links: Original Bluemarble site and visibleearth (where this data was taken from), with many more images (clouds, ice etc.).
